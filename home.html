Why Habitomic?

We help you find and form the best habit based on your personality type.
Let’s make a new journey together.
Habit Formation Journey
Personality Type is the Key

No two people are alike; people have different needs and desires. Here, the communication is based on your personality type determined by psychometric tools.

Evaluate your habit

Psychometric test can help you find out whether the habit is what you need. Of course, If the habit suits you, you are more likely to stick to it.

Find your starting point

Start your journey by breaking down your selected habit to minihabits, daily routines that just take a few minutes. Minihabits build up a new habit step by step.
